Projects

  • Diabetes Risk Prediction Using ML – In this project, my team and I explored how machine learning models can be used to predict diabetes risk using behavioral and demographic data from the CDC’s BRFSS dataset. We applied K-Nearest Neighbors, Decision Trees, and Logistic Regression after addressing class imbalance, conducting exploratory data analysis, and selecting key features. Our goal was to evaluate how well each model could identify individuals at risk for diabetes, especially in the context of improving public health outcomes.
